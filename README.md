---

# 📖 About This Repository

This repository documents my research journey into **robotic grasping**, **robot manipulation**, and **AI-driven robotic perception** through an in-depth study of influential research papers.

Rather than providing brief summaries, each paper is carefully analyzed to understand:

- Research motivation and problem formulation
- Technical architecture
- Mathematical foundations
- Neural network design
- Training methodology
- Experimental validation
- Strengths and limitations
- Industrial applications
- Future research directions

The objective is to build a comprehensive knowledge base for advanced research in robotic manipulation and safe Human–Robot Interaction.
